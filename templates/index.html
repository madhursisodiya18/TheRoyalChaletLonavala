About The Royal Chalet

The Royal Chalet is a luxurious mountain villa just minutes from the main road yet blissfully secluded from noise. Blending traditional charm with modern comfort, it features a stunning living room, kitchen, and bedrooms that open to a serene pool with lush green views.

With four elegant ensuite bedrooms and a cozy terrace overlooking the hills, it’s perfect for family getaways or intimate gatherings. Guests can enjoy delicious home-style meals or evening barbeque sessions by the pool under the stars, creating unforgettable moments.

Whether you wish to work, unwind, or celebrate, The Royal Chalet has it all. Nestled in the heart of Lonavala Khandala, the “Jewel of Sahyadri,” it’s surrounded by lush valleys, tranquil lakes, and waterfalls—an idyllic escape from city life.
